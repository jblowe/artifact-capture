Checklists for using the app

These lists will help you capture ceramics data efficiently. The workflow described covers the regular analysis process including filling out the paper Data Sheet and entering data and photos using the app. The workflow is as follows:

  • Set up your table with what you will need (pencil, Data Sheets, lights, magnifying glass, diameter charts, tablet, etc.) Everyone should have their own tablet.
  • Fetch a bag of sherds to work on.
  • Dump them out, arrange them, tag them, measure them, photograph them, enter the data in the paper sheet and the tablet. This is the part of the workflow that is described by the checklists below.
  • Return the sherds to the bag.
  • Tear off the lower part of the T-Tag and place it inside the bag.
  • Tie up the bag as it was, and tie the upper part of the T-Tag to the outside of the bag.
  • Place the original bag in a new plastic ZipLoc bag and seal it.
  • Place the completed bag in the appropriate box.

Adding data and images

Add Bag record

  1. Select a bag to work on (check with Karen to find the next bag)
  2. Get a fresh data sheet and put the name of the Recorders and today‚Äôs Date at the top.

  5. Enter the bag and recorder info into the tablet: Recorders, Season, Context, Excavation Date, and if needed, Notes.
  6. Open the bag at your table and arrange all the sherds nicely on the white surface.
  7. Ensure that the T-Tag, internal bag label, external bag label, and scale are all present and neatly arranged.

Take a photo with the tablet

  1. On the tablet, click the Take Photo button.
  2. Hold the tablet directly over the sherds and tags, at a height that allows you to capture all the sherds, tags, and scale. Make sure the lighting is as good as possible.
  3. Click the white round button on the table to take the picture.
  4. If the picture is OK, click the Checkmark button.
  5. If the picture is not OK, click the Retake button and go back to step 3. to retake the photo.
  6. You should now check the following:
    1. All fields entered correctly. Double check!
    2. An image filename like ‚Äúnnnnnnnnnnnn.jpg‚Äù appears next to the Take Photo button.
  7. Very Important: Click Add image to save the image and data!

Add Artifact Records

  1. Sort artifacts into Rims, Bases, and Body sections.
  2. For Rims, measure the Rim Diameter, determine the Temper, Typology Number, and Surface Treatment and enter these 4 values for each Rim in the Datasheet.
  3. For Body and Base sherds, arrange the sherds by Surface Treatment.
  4. For each Surface Treatment, note the Treatment types and the number of sherds of that type on the Data Sheet.
  5. For interesting Sherds or groups of Sherds of any type, create an Artifact records using the tablet:
    1. Click on the Artifacts tab on the tablet.
    2. Click ‚ÄúCopy from Bags‚Äù (which copies the Context information from the Bag tab)
    3. Verify that the Context and Recorder information is correct.
    4. Enter the Surface Treatment and any other relevant information into the tablet.
    5. Take a photo of the sherd or sherds following the directions above ‚ÄúTake a photo with the tablet‚Äù.
    6. You can take multiple photos for the same sherd or group. Just repeat the Take a Photo process.
    7. More is better! We want photos of infrequent Surface Treatments, Tempers, Typologies!
